Our Mission

Imagine a world where, you, the student are regarded not as source of revenue but what you should be considered, a student. With LibroZeus, we hope to give college students an affordable and convenient internet retail service where they can save, earn profit, and market their used textbooks.

How It Works

List It

Log into LibroZeus and find the book you are trying to sell. Click "sell my copy" and you are done.

We ship it

After selling your book, drop off the book at the school mail center. We will take care of the rest.

Get Paid

As soon as you sell the book you get paid and the great news is that our fees are not greedy like Amazon or Ebay's fees!
