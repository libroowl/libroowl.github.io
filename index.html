What is Libroowl?

Our Mission

Imagine a world where, you, the student are regarded not as source of revenue but what you should be considered, a student. With Libroowl, we hope to give college students an affordable and convenient internet retail service where they can save, earn profit, and market their used textbooks.

Solution

Libroowl strives to liberate students from the overpriced textbook industry by creating a student community driven platform where students can buy or sell textbooks at reasonable prices.

How Selling Works

List It

Log into Libroowl and find the book you are trying to sell. Click "sell my copy" and you are done.

We ship it

After selling your book, drop off the book at the school mail center. We will take care of the rest.

Get Paid

As soon as you sell the book you get paid. No need to wait or have a huge chunk of your earnings taken away.

What About Buying?

Search for the book

Log into Libroowl and seach for your desired book by entering the ISBN, title, or class into the search bar or using the naviagtion bar.

Click and Buy

Libroowl is secure and does not store your credit card information for transactions. Use paypal to buy the item and the book is yours.

Pick up your book

Head down to the mail center and check your mailbox. Your textbook will be waiting.
